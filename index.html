A UNIQUE BRITISH BOARDING SCHOOL

IN THE HEART OF KENYA

1927

Pembroke House follows the British Common entrance curiculum and is one of the oldest international boarding prep schools in Kenya. Founded in 1927 it has been educating boys and girls for over 85 years. the school has been a charitable trust since 1959, and all income is re-invisted into the improvement of education for the childred.
